Job Details: Primary Teacher - Year 3

Primary Teacher - Year 3

Location: Bristol | Contract: Permanent | Start Date: September 2025

About the Role:

A fantastic opportunity for a passionate and dedicated Year 3 Primary Teacher to join a thriving and supportive school in Bristol. The school is committed to providing a nurturing and stimulating learning environment for all its pupils. You will be responsible for planning and delivering engaging lessons, assessing student progress, and contributing to the wider school community.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Plan and deliver high-quality lessons in line with the national curriculum.
  • Assess, monitor, and report on the progress of pupils.
  • Create a positive and inclusive classroom environment.
  • Communicate effectively with parents and guardians.
  • Participate in school meetings and professional development.

Requirements:

  • Qualified Teacher Status (QTS) in the UK.
  • Experience teaching Year 3 or Key Stage 2.
  • Strong knowledge of the primary national curriculum.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Commitment to safeguarding and promoting the welfare of children.

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary.
  • Supportive leadership and a collaborative team.
  • Opportunities for professional growth and development.
  • Access to a wide range of resources.
