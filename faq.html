Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services for parents, schools, and tutors.

For Parents

How do I find a tutor for my child?

You can use our search bar on the homepage to find tutors by subject, location, and other criteria. You can also contact us directly for personalized recommendations.

What are your tutoring rates?

Tutoring rates vary depending on the tutor's experience, subject, and location. Please contact us for detailed pricing information.

Are your tutors qualified?

Yes, all our tutors are highly qualified, experienced, and undergo a rigorous vetting process, including DBS checks.

For Schools

How can Tutelage Services help my school?

We provide comprehensive recruitment solutions for permanent, temporary, and supply teaching staff, including SEN specialists. We also assist with international teacher recruitment.

What is your vetting process for school staff?

We adhere to strict safeguarding guidelines, conducting thorough background checks, professional references, and DBS checks for all candidates.

For Tutors

How do I register as a tutor?

You can register your interest on our Job page. Our team will then guide you through the application and vetting process.

What support do you offer tutors?

We provide ongoing support, including access to resources, professional development opportunities, and a dedicated consultant to assist with your placements.

Tutelage Services

We are a leading education recruitment agency, helping teachers and support staff find their next role in schools across the UK.
